The Rhythm of Mobility

Selected official mobility milestones, 1997–2025

This overview combines selected official milestones reported in different but related measures — pre-2020 average daily cross-boundary passenger traffic and post-2020 annual control-point passenger movements. It is meant to show the changing rhythm of mobility, not a perfectly uniform statistical series.
